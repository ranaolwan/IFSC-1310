My Life Journey

Where it Began

My name is Rana Olwan. I am Palestinian and American girl. I graduated from Mills University Studies High School in Little Rock, AR. I am 19 years old. I am student at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock majoring in Computer Science and I want to become Web Developer in the Future. I will share a little bit about my story and the accompleshments I did since I was little until now.

I grew up with my mom's family when my dad traveled. My mom is the reason for making me strong enough to go through this Life They tought me what does life, love, success, respect, and kindeness means. Life was not easy for us. Going to school everyday I have to listen how other girls waiting their dads to dinner and whenver they had conflict or they are sick theis dads came and took them while I amsitting aside have nothing to say about my dad. I just see him behined the screen for 20 to 30 minutes every once in a while, but on the other hand I was the only one who dressed well and living better than them. My dad's motivation and support encourage me to move on and be at the head of my classes each year. I knew he will happy from that so I did it. "It is dad!!" It was the first time I saw my dad on 2013 during the winter and it was snowing that day. It was starnge feeling but at the same time happy that my dad is here.

I graduated from my Middle School on the first place and was the graduation speaker on that day. My performancce and support I had from my familt encouraged me that on High School I'll do my best. My old High School used to be from 5th until 12th grade. I studyed a lot and I make it through 9th grade with all A's and hnors. The story began when I started my 9th grade when I participated in the AI Competition around the world. I made an application using AI which decide based on the data I collected it if the person have Flatfoot or not. I was the leader and made it with mmy teem. We got the 5th palce at the Directory of Education of my city. That was the beganing of the story.

Moving to the United States
